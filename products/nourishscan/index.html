Flagship product

NourishScan

NourishScan is a consumer health informatics application that helps people understand food choices. It surfaces nutrition insights, trade-offs, and patterns over time, supporting gradual improvement while respecting individual autonomy.

Open NourishScan Explore Health Informatics

How it works

  • Upload data
  • AI extracts structured signals
  • Insights explain nutrition and trade-offs
  • Track patterns and progress over time

What it optimizes for

  • Decision quality over rigid prescriptions
  • Gradual improvement, one change at a time
  • Interpretability and user control
  • Longitudinal insight, not one-off scoring

Where it fits

NourishScan sits in our Health Informatics vertical. It is the central product we are building around as we expand into personalized learning and scientific discovery tooling.
